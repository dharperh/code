WHAT ARE THE AREAS OF STUDY?

As a Launch Code student you will learn how to work with Javascript, HTML, CSS, Git, Angular, and Visual Studio. You will have sample learning projects and assignments that will help with building skill sets in the various areas of study. Instructor trainings will be video taped so that students can review when needed.

Warning!!!! Take heed to the drop assignment(s).

SideBar-- I have no experience with any of this. I like a challenge,... but, there is so much to learn and in my case, so little time.

INSIGHT ON WHAT TO EXPECT

As a student, you can expect to dedicate many hours to study and learning. How much time depends on whether or not you have prior training and/or knowledge of the course offerings. The instructor training and TA support is very good. Your success will be determined by your motivation level and the amount of time available for study. Expect to work long and hard. Anything worth having is worth the effort it will take to attain it.

HOW WILL THE LAUNCH CODE COURSE BENEFIT ME?

The knowledge and training gained is priceless. You will be equipped to learn other computer languages and gain knowledge to branch off into numerous other areas of computer technologies. Once you have the knowledge it is up to you to find the career path that is right for you.

Good Luck Launch Coder! Get ready for blast off into a successful future!!!!
